About

Hi. My name is Rob and I love to make web apps! I use all the tools I know: html, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap, Node.js, and many others; for the full stack. Check out my stuff I did in class. There’s a couple of games, an app using persistent storage on the back end, and a search app called Concert Concierge intended to allow the user to find out-of-town events and travel and hotel bookings all in one. All apps are proof-of-concept version. Once you’ve tried it, contact me and let me know how I did. I can’t wait to hear from you! This portfolio was made with CSS3 and Bootstrap stylings.
