About

If you have a business, profession, hobby, art portfolio, or anything to promote, you need to be on the Internet. Having a social media presence is important, but if you want your stuff to be yours and completely under your control, you need a website. Having a dedicated web developer to design and create your site or app is the best way to present yourself under your own terms. Templators and site-creation services are helpful, but having your own web developer ensures total control of your content.

Hi, my name is Rob and I’m a web developer. I can build the site or app you want at a quality rate that is tendered toward your goals and needs. I use HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node Modules, and React.js. I can edit photos and create written content. Anything you need to complete your vision, I can make it happen. If you need server, database, or backend functionality, I got you covered with skills in SQL database solutions, MongoDB, AJAX, and more.

Please review my content in my Portfolio. Apps in portfolio are for demonstration purposes only. Your final product will be fully functional and ready to launch in a timely manner. I’m looking forward to hearing from you soon.
